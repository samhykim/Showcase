Find Your Showcase Lineup


  Having a stressful time making a dance showcase lineup? Well stress no more! 
  Follow these steps and you can find a showcase lineup in no time.
  1. Upload your spreadsheet with the link below. There are three options:
    a. Orientation - click the checkbox if you put the dance team names on the first row. 
       (each column corresponds to a team). Don't check the checkbox if the dance team
       names are on the first column (each row corresponds to a team).
    b. Separator - if you used a csv file, write ','. If you use a .tsv file, write '\t'
    c. Max Conflicts - indicate the maximum number of conflicts you will allow for the showcase.
       Default is 0.
  2. Choose an opening act. A list will automatically generate arbitrarily. Delete this list. 
     You will have re-enter any teams you want to already fix in the showcase lineup again. 
  3. Enter any other teams you want to have in a specific order. Make sure teams are spelled with exact
     spelling, lower/uppercase, and punctuation. Then click Find Lineup.
  Afterwards, 6 randomly generated lineups that satisfies the contraints will appear on the right. Feel
  free to play around with the list and change the order to find new combinations for lineups.
